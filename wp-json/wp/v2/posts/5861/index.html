Chiropractic is a treatment method that has moved from more of an alternative treatment to widespread mainstream acceptance over the past few decades. 1) Headaches<\/h3>\n

We all get headaches from time to time. Some are mild, some are more severe, but all of them have the potential to disrupt your day. Whether it is a tension headache caused by hours of sitting at a desk or a migraine brought on by a specific health condition, when the pain starts it is difficult to focus on anything else.<\/p>\n

State College chiropractors help relieve headache pain on a regular basis. A lot of the time the pain you feel in your head is actually being caused by misalignment of the vertebrae in your spine. After a gentle adjustment and proper maintenance, many patients report no more pain and no more need for pain medication.<\/p>\n

2) Shoulder Pain<\/h3>\n

Shoulder pain is a problem that far too many people deal with.\u00a0 Pain in the shoulder can be referred pain from the spinal misalignments, or it can be caused by a misalignment in the shoulder.<\/p>\n

No matter what specific type of shoulder pain you have, State College chiropractors help relieve both the pain and any range of motion issues that are present. Our team will help to access your condition and give you the best advice we can on what you can do to remedy the situation.<\/p>\n

3) Lower Back Pain<\/h3>\n

If headaches and shoulder pain are among the most common conditions for the general public then lower back pain is probably at the head of the pack. One of the most common reasons for lumbar pain is from misaligned spinal segments.<\/p>\n

Our Healthy Valley Chiropractic team can help the pain and reduced mobility associated with lumbar strains and sprains and help patients get back to their normal routines.
