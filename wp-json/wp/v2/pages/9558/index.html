UPDATED: December 2017<\/p>\n

General<\/strong><\/p>\n

Healthy Valley Chiropractic strives to ensure that its services are accessible to people with disabilities. Healthy Valley Chiropractic\u00a0has invested a significant amount of resources to help ensure that its website is made easier to use and more accessible for people with disabilities, with the strong belief that every person has the right to live with dignity, equality, comfort and independence.<\/p>\n

\n

Accessibility<\/strong><\/p>\n

Healthy Valley Chiropractic makes available the\u00a0UserWay Website<\/a> Accessibility Widget\u00a0that is powered by a dedicated accessibility server. The software allows Healthy Valley Chiropractic\u00a0to improve its compliance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.0).<\/p>\n

\n

Enabling the Accessibility Menu<\/strong><\/p>\n

The\u00a0accessibility menu can be enabled by clicking the accessibility menu icon that appears on the corner of the page. After triggering the accessibility menu, please wait a moment for the accessibility menu to load in its entirety.<\/p>\n

\n

Disclaimer<\/strong><\/p>\n

Healthy Valley Chiropractic continues its efforts to constantly improve the accessibility of its site and services in the belief that it is our collective moral obligation to allow seamless, accessible and unhindered use also for those of us with disabilities.<\/p>\n

Despite our efforts to make all pages and content on\u00a0www.healthyvalleychiropractic.com<\/a>\u00a0fully accessible, some content may not have yet been fully adapted to the strictest accessibility standards. This may be a result of not having found or identified the most appropriate technological solution.<\/p>\n

\n

Here For You<\/strong><\/p>\n

If you are experiencing difficulty with any content on\u00a0www.healthyvalleychiropractic.com<\/a> or require assistance with any part of our site, please contact us during normal business hours as detailed below and we will be happy to assist.<\/p>\n

\n

Contact Us<\/strong><\/p>\n
